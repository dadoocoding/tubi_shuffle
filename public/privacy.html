Tubi Shuffle TV Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

Tubi Shuffle TV is a local-only Chrome extension that helps users create pseudo-TV channels from Tubi URLs. Playback remains on official Tubi pages.

Information We Collect

Tubi Shuffle TV does not collect, sell, rent, transmit, or share personal information.

The extension stores channel data locally in your browser, including:

  • Channel names you create
  • Tubi URLs you add
  • Local playback queue state
  • Local episode progression
  • Extension settings

This data is stored using Chrome extension local storage on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

Tubi Shuffle TV does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your Tubi account credentials
  • Your full browsing history
  • Video files or streams
  • Payment information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking data

How the Extension Uses Permissions

Tubi Shuffle TV requests access to Tubi pages so it can:

  • Open official Tubi playback pages
  • Detect when a video element is present
  • Detect when playback appears to finish
  • Attempt normal page playback controls when a selected item loads

The extension does not block, skip, modify, replace, or interfere with Tubi ads. It does not restream, download, or copy Tubi video content.

Data Sharing

Tubi Shuffle TV does not send your channel data or playback state to any remote server.

If you export a playlist, the extension creates a JSON file on your device. You control whether and how that file is shared.

Third-Party Services

Playback occurs on official Tubi pages. Tubi's own privacy practices are governed by Tubi's privacy policy, not this extension.

Data Deletion

You can delete channels from the extension popup. You can also remove all extension data by uninstalling the extension or clearing its site and extension storage through Chrome.

Changes

This policy may be updated if the extension's behavior changes. Any future feature that uses remote services, analytics, or cloud sync should be reflected here before release.
